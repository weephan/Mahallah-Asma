2022/2023

Let's Tadabbur 1.0

1 April 2022

Tadabbur, the reflective pondering upon Quranic verses, is highly encouraged in Islam for a deeper understanding. In light of the holy month of Ramadan, Ustaz Halil Ibrahim has been invited to share insights into Surah An-Nas, a surah frequently recited during this period. The goal is to enhance comprehension and meaning when engaging with this verse.

Talk Nuzul Al-Quran

19 April 2022

Explore the fascinating journey of how the Qur'an was revealed in stages to Prophet Muhammad, culminating in the complete scripture we read today. Delve into the greatness and wonders of the Al-Quran, uncovering its profound messages and wisdom.

Ramadhan Sharing 4.0

26 April 2022

Sis Zahirah shared an insightful video discussing the countdown to the end of Ramadan. The event emphasized the importance of utilizing the remaining time in Ramadan to obtain Allah's forgiveness and reap the abundant rewards this sacred month offers to every Muslim.

Smar Al-Fatihah

21 May 2022

The 'SMART AL FATIHAH' program, organized by the JKQ Club in collaboration with Mahallah Asma and PERKIM, successfully provided a platform for teaching the correct recitation and reflection (tadabbur) of Surah Al-Fatihah. The event aimed to enhance knowledge and proficiency in reciting this essential Quranic chapter.

Mahallah Campaign

Awards

Mahallah Asma' is well-known as an active Mahallah that never miss a chance to participate in any competition or events.

List of Mahallah Asma' Awards

Asma' Fundraising

Asma' fundraising is the most outstanding achievement, that successfully reached more than half of thousand Malaysia Ringgit in a month. It was an excellent programme organized by bureau Entrepreneurship with the help of MRC and BRC during IIUM Sports Carnival. Massive supports were received from IIUM community along the process of fundraising. Not to forget, Mahallah Asma's residents were also very supportive and helpful.

‘Tandas Bersih’ Challenge

On early July 2020, Mahallah Asma' took an opportunity to participate in a programme organized by University Putra Malaysia (UPM) that is open to public. The ‘Tandas Bersih’ challenge was an amazing experience for Mahallah Asma' to uncover the talent and uniqueness of Mahallah Asma' in competing with several other great competitors.

Inter-Mahallah Cultural Week 2021

Inter-Mahallah Cultural Week 2021 was an online competition among all masala in IIUM. This competition was divided into 7 categories, i.e. tarannum, pantun, fashion show, traditional song, nasyid, drama, and poetry recitation. Mahallah Asma' took place in all categories except traditional song and painting. Among all the categories that Asma' took place, we managed to get 3rd place in fashion show who was being participated in one of Asma's residents, Sr, Aisyah Humaira.

Mental Health Corner

Mental Health Corner during Mental health month and we got 4th place.

The most polite MRC

Mahallah Asma' were given the title of ‘The most polite MRC’ during the McLead programme though it is an adventurous camping with many challenges in building leadership, teamwork , explorace and outdoor activities. MRC of Mahallah Asma' actively participate with others during the programme.

Champion of Traditional Game

Champion of Traditional Game: ‘Batu Seremban’ during IIUM Indigenous Festival. It was particpated by residents of Mahallah Asma' and competed with other Mahallahs
